Observables

- Observable helps us to bring the asynchrous, dynamic or subject behaviour in our application. Observables are like a river where the flow of the water is a continuos process. If we connect a pipeline to get / subscribe the water / data from a tap, we will call the subscribe function. & when we don't need it we can simply unsubscribe.
- allows you to work with asynchronous code
- It needs to be imported from rxjs. It gives us various methods to transform the output by using pipe & then various methods into it like map, filter, buffer, bufferCount etc
